### **II. Pre-Test Questions (2 Minutes)**

**(Goal: Establish context and experience level)**

| Question | Moderator Prompts | Participant Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1. App Usage | How often do you typically **sign up for new mobile apps**? (Daily, weekly, rarely?) | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |
| 2. Pain Points | When you sign up for a new service, what is the **most frustrating thing** that usually happens? | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |
| 3. Technology | On a scale of 1 (Low) to 5 (High), how comfortable are you with **using new technology**? | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |

---

### **III. Core Task (5 Minutes)**

**(Goal: Observe success/failure and points of friction in the sign-up flow.)**

### **IV. Post-Task Questions (3 Minutes)**

**(Goal: Probe into the 'why' behind the user's observed behavior.)**

| Question | Moderator Prompts | Participant Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1. Clarity | What was the **most confusing or unclear part** of that sign-up process? | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |
| 2. Trust/Security | Did the process make you feel confident that your **password was secure**? Why or why not? | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |
| 3. Suggestions | If you could change **one thing** about this flow, what would it be? | **[Moderator: Record Answer]** |
